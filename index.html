👋About

A little about me.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Information and Communication Engineering at UESTC.

I study how RF models can stay reliable across changing environments and run efficiently on resource-constrained hardware. My work spans RF fingerprinting, domain adaptation, efficient inference, and RF language models.

🛠️Practical systems

SpectraEdge SDR

A working RF edge-AI prototype built around ADRV9009, ZU19EG FPGA, and Jetson Orin NX.

It connects real RF acquisition, FPGA preprocessing, and edge inference in one system.

  1. 01
    ADRV9009

    RF acquisition and digital baseband conversion

  2. 02
    ZU19EG FPGA

    Real-time STFT, feature extraction, and preprocessing

  3. 03
    Jetson Orin NX

    Edge-side deep learning and intelligent inference

RF Signal→ADRV9009→FPGA→Orin NX→AI Result
